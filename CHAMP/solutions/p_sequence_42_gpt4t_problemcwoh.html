This problem is a variation of the classic Josephus problem, which is a theoretical problem related to a certain elimination process. In the classic Josephus problem, people are standing in a circle waiting to be executed. Starting with a certain person, you count around the circle and every k-th person will be killed until one person remains. The problem is to find the position in which you should stand in order to be the last survivor.
