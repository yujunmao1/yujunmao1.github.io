We can write $g(x, y) = \sum_{i=0}^n a_i x^i y^j$ for some coefficients $a_i$. Then,
\begin{align*}
f(x, y) &= (x-y)g(x, y) \\
&= (x-y) \sum_{i=0}^n a_i x^i y^j \\
&= \sum_{i=0}^n a_i (x^{i+1} - y^{i+1}) y^j \\
&= \sum_{i=0}^n a_i x^{i+1} y^j - \sum_{i=0}^n a_i y^{i+1} y^j \\
