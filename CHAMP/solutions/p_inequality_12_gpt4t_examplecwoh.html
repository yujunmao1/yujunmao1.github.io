It states that when you have two sequences of real numbers, each sorted in the same order (either both non-decreasing or both non-increasing), the sum of the products of corresponding terms is maximized. \sum_{k=1}^{n} \frac{k}{(n-k+1)^2}

\sum_{j=n}^{1} \frac{n-j+1}{j^2}
