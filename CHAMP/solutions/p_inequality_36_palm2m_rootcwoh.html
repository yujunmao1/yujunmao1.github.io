\begin{align*}

&= \sqrt{(a-c-\sqrt{ab}\sqrt{cd})^2}-1 \\
&= \sqrt{(a-c-\sqrt{(ab)(cd)})^2}-1.
\end{align*}Equality occurs when $a=c$ and $b=d$, so the smallest value is $\boxed{\sqrt{(a-c-\sqrt{(ab)(cd)})^2}-1}$.
