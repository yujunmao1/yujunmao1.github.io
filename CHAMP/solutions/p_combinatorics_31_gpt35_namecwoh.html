The formula for combinations is:

C(n, r) = n! / (r! * (n - r)!)

Where:
- "n" represents the total number of items in the set.
- "r" represents the number of items to be chosen from the set.
- "!" denotes the factorial operation, which means multiplying a number by all positive integers less than itself down to 1.
