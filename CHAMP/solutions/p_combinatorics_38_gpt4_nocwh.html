The number of ways to select two boys from four is given by the combination formula C(n, r) = n! / [r!(n-r)!], where n is the total number of items, r is the number of items to choose, and ! denotes factorial. 

So, C(4, 2) = 4! / [2!(4-2)!] = (4*3*2*1) / [(2*1)*(2*1)] = 6 ways.
