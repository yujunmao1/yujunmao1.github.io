- \( n \) is the total number of items.
- \( k \) is the number of items to be chosen.
- \( n! \) (n factorial) is the product of all positive integers from 1 to \( n \) (with \( 0! \) defined as 1).
- \( k! \) and \( (n-k)! \) are the factorials of \( k \) and \( n-k \), respectively.

\[ C(10, 3) = \binom{10}{3} = \frac{10!}{3!(10-3)!} = \frac{10!}{3!7!} = \frac{10 \times 9 \times 8}{3 \times 2 \times 1} = 120 \]
