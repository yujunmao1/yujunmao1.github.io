\[

\[
GM = \sqrt[n]{a_1 \cdot a_2 \cdot \ldots \cdot a_n}

The AM-GM inequality is particularly useful because it gives a way to compare the averages of a set of numbers in a manner that is independent of the distribution of values within the set.
