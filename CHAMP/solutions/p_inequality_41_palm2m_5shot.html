Let a_i≥1 for all i. What is the minimum value of (1+a_1)(1+a_2)...(1+a_n)/(1+a_1+a_2+...+a_n) as an expression of n?
Step-wise solution:
1. Let the minimum value be $m$.
2. We have \begin{align*}
m &= \frac{(1+a_1)(1+a_2)...(1+a_n)}{1+a_1+a_2+...+a_n} \\\
&= \frac{\prod_{i=1}^n (1+a_i)}{\sum_{i=1}^n a_i} \\\
&\geq \frac{\prod_{i=1}^n 2}{\sum_{i=1}^n a_i} \\\
&= \frac{2^n}{\sum_{i=1}^n a_i}.
\end{align*}
3. Equality occurs when $a_1=a_2=\cdots=a_n=2$.
4. Thus, the minimum value is $\boxed{\frac{2^n}{\sum_{i=1}^n a_i}}$.
